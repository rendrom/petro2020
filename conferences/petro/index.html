Dear colleagues!

We are honored to invite you to the XIII National Petrographic Conference «Petrology and geodynamics of geological processes» to be held in Irkutsk and organized by the Institute of Geochemistry SB RAS, Institute of the Earth’s crust and National Petrographic Committee. .

Irkutsk

07-13
september
2020г
First circular

Topics

(scientific sessions)

  1. Magmatism of diverse geodynamic settings.
  2. Metamorphism and lithogenesis.
  3. Petrogenesis and mineralogy of the Earth deep shells.
  4. Magmatism, metasomatism and ore formation.
  5. Isotope-and-geochemical studies in petrology.
  6. Experimental and physic-chemical simulation of geologic processes.
  7. Rock classification.
  8. Petrography in education and interdisciplinary studies.
  9. Magmatic and metamorphic complexes of the Baikal region.
