Registration fee amounts to 3000 R. It includes the case with a set of conference materials, coffee breaks. Students and graduate students are exempt from organizational fee.

The registration fee may be paid either by (1) transferring it to the account of IGC SB RAS or (2) with cash when registering upon arrival. Please indicate the preferred option in the registration form of Personal page at the site. In the first case, it is necessary to conclude a Contract prior to June 15, 2020. Please find its form and info on the cashless payment at the conference site http://petro2020.igc.irk.ru/en/registration
